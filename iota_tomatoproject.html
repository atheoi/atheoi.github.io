Tomato Project

Take the spotlight in this game about staying on stage. Collect red tomatoes for points, avoid rotten tomatoes to stay alive.

Use the tilt control from your phone to lead tomato around the stage. Simple rules and easy to grasp control, if your having any trouble we've got a tutorial ready to answer the questions you'll have for Tomato.

Tomato Project also features google play function to connect with your friend in a 1v1 showdown.

Beware of the rotten purple tomato and the fast rolling phantom tomato, getting hit by any one of these could be fatal. Have fun and Tomato thank you for helping her!
