Cloud Kingdom TEST

Take to the skies in this 3D action adventure card game.

Cloud Kingdom is a game where choices mean everything. Choose and build your own unique play style by mixing and matching collectable cards, which grant you the ability to customise your spells and attacks.

This is our most ambitious project that has been refined through multiple iterations. The mechanic is a new take on a traditional 3d action game with a strategy card game twist.
