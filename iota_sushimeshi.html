Sushi Meshi

This simple game about collecting food and making sushi will get you hooked! (hah, get it, hooked...)

Sashi the sushi chef had just opened her new shop, which she always dreamed of. Use the hooks grab the raw material for points and also gain multiple hooks to grab more.

Customers will pop in once in a while and complete their request by hooking the necessary materials and gain extra pints and extra reputation

Satisfy the customer, keep Sashi and her shop rolling!
